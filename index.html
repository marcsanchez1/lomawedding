Our Story

For most couples, there is love at first sight. But for Lauren and Marc, there was an inexplicable attraction, slow burning intrigue, and a knowing in their hearts that they would one day be in love.

On January 12, 2012, Lauren started her first day of work at PowerChord. When Marc introduced himself to her that day, the first thing that Lauren noticed was his smile. Marc first noticed the way that Lauren would walk and talk. He fondly remembers that her intelligence and authenticity stood out. They became acquainted over many months and their friendship began to develop into a romance.

At first, there was hesitaton. Marc is 14 years older than Lauren and the father of two beautiful children, Marc and Karina. Naturally, Lauren battled in her mind with the idea of dating an older man with two kids. Marc was also hesitant about their age difference, but neither one could shake the idea that there was something special between them. And then came Nyla.

In April of 2013, Marc’s brother, Eddie, found a 10-month-old abandoned Pit bull. After a failed attempt to find her owner, Marc was thinking about making this sweet pup his very own. As soon as Lauren saw a photo of her, she knew that the dog's name would be Nyla. That sealed the deal for Marc and he picked her up that evening.

Marc believes that Lauren may not have continued to come around if it weren’t for Nyla. And he may have been right! LOL. The handsome man with a cute puppy combination really pulled at Lauren’s heart strings. One month later, on May 12, 2013, Lauren and Marc officially began their journey as a couple. Choosing to follow their hearts and taking a total leap of faith on love was arguably the best decision they have ever made.

Over the years, Lauren and Marc have experienced many of life’s precious moments together. Lauren lost four of her grandparents, while also learning how to be a step mother to Marc and Karina. As challenging as it was for both of us, we knew in our hearts that it would bind our eternal love with many pleasant and challenging experiences. They moved in together after a year of dating, then later purchased their first home together on February 9, 2018. They have traveled to Asheville, Washington D.C., San Francisco, Blue Ridge and Lauren’s hometown of Dundee. They have celebrated birthdays, promotions, and the high school graduation of their son Marc.

Their favorite memories, though, are the most simple moments they have experienced together: watching sunsets and dolphins playing in the water,relaxing on a hammock and reading a book, drinking coffee and having breakfast in the morning, talking about their day then catching up on favorite shows at night. Lauren and Marc pride themselves on being involved in every aspect of each other’s life, discussing and supporting one another every step of the way.

And as time goes on, their life together only gets sweeter. For Lauren and Marc, having an intimate wedding surrounded by their closest family and friends will truly be the icing on top, and a moment they already know that they will never forget.

He is her first and only love. She is his last and best love.

Ana Polak Atelier
