Our Story

For many couples, there is love at first sight. But for Lauren and Marc, there was an inexplicable attraction and a knowing in their hearts that they would one day be in love.

When Marc introduced himself to Lauren, the first thing that she noticed was his smile. Marc first noticed the way Lauren would speed-walk to and from her desk. He thought it was funny. He fondly remembers that her intelligence and authenticity stood out. They became acquainted over many months and their friendship began to develop into a romance. And when Nyla came into the picture, that’s when Lauren and Marc made it “official.”

Nyla was a 10 week old abandoned Pitbull who Marc decided to rescue. She was the cutest thing, and Marc swears that’s what sealed the deal for Lauren. He may be right because the handsome man and cute puppy combination really pulled at Lauren’s heart strings.

Since May of 2013, Lauren and Marc have experienced many of life’s precious moments together, including the special bond they have with their kids Marc and Karina. They have purchased a home, traveled the country, and envisioned their future. But their favorite memories are the simplest moments: laughing with the kids, playing with Nyla, watching sunsets and dolphins in the water, relaxing on a hammock and reading a book, drinking coffee and having breakfast in the morning, talking about their day then catching up on favorite shows at night.

Lauren and Marc pride themselves on being involved in every aspect of each other’s life, supporting each other every step of the way. They also agree that choosing to follow their hearts and taking a total leap of faith on love was the best decision they have ever made.

As the time goes on, their life together only gets sweeter and they can’t wait to have an intimate wedding surrounded by their closest family and friends.

He is her first love. She is his last love.

Ana Polak Atelier
